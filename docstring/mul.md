---
title: "multiplication (*)"
description: "calculates the product of a number sequence"
names: ["*"]
usage: "(* form*)"
tags: ["math", "number"]
---

Calculates the product of a set of numbers.

#### An Example

```scheme
(\* 9 10 23)       ;; returns 2070
(\* 50 12.6 34.8)  ;; returns 21924
(\*)               ;; returns 1
```
